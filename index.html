    Summary!

    Mr. Thoeurn Phen is a dedicated and versatile professional with over a decade of hands-on experience in operations, administration, IT, and leadership. With strong interpersonal skills and a deep understanding of Cambodian markets, he thrives in both corporate and community-based environments.
